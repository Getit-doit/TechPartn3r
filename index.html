What Is Eviction Management?

The process is more than filing papers.

We position the service as a partner rather than a basic set-out service or collections shop. The focus is coordinated communication, demand delivery, filing support, case updates, and practical help moving landlords through a difficult timeline with fewer avoidable mistakes.

Every page is designed to make the process easier to understand and faster to act on when time, rent loss, and property risk are all rising at once.

How The Process Works

Three practical stages.

The process moves through demand, filing, and court. Pricing and county-specific fees should always be confirmed directly with the business.

Step 1

Demand / Pay-or-quit notice

Initial service is presented publicly from $50, with additional demand delivery fees based on location and other factors.

  • Notice preparation
  • Call, email, and mail outreach
  • Direct communication handoff
Step 2

File the dispossessory

Case filing support, service verification, and updates while the matter moves toward the defendant’s answer and hearing schedule.

  • County filing support
  • Marshal or process service coordination
  • Attorney update flow
Step 3

Court and next actions

Attorney representation and testimony planning depend on the county, the tenant’s response, and the facts of the case.

  • One court appearance may be included depending on the fee structure
  • Tenant answer and counterclaim review
  • Writ and set-out pricing handled separately
