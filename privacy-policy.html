Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Evict Them For Me, together with Southern Real Estate Services Group, LLC, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you share when you visit this site, submit an intake request, use a portal, request a consultation, or communicate by phone, email, or text message.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, email address, mailing address, property address, tenant information, lease details, payment status, court-related details, and any other information relevant to eviction management, real estate, or property-related services. We may also collect browser, device, IP address, and website interaction data.

How We Use Information

We may use collected information to respond to inquiries, open or manage matters, deliver demand communications, coordinate filings, support legal or service-provider workflows, communicate service updates, improve site performance, maintain records, and satisfy operational, regulatory, or fraud-prevention needs.

Cookies and Tracking

This site may use cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies to understand site usage, improve performance, and support permitted marketing activities. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

How Information May Be Shared

We do not sell personal information for money. Information may be shared with attorneys, courts, process servers, marshals, property-related service providers, technology vendors, messaging vendors, analytics providers, and other parties where reasonably necessary to deliver requested services, comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or prevent misuse.

Mobile Information Sharing

SMS opt-in information and mobile phone numbers collected for text messaging will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Mobile information may be shared only with service providers that help us send text messages or operate related communications on our behalf.

TCPA and SMS Consent

By providing your phone number to Evict Them For Me or Southern Real Estate Services Group, LLC, you expressly consent to receive telephone calls and text messages, including account notifications, customer care messages, scheduling communications, case-related updates, and marketing messages where permitted by law. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.

Data Security and Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain records, satisfy legal or regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your communication preferences may be directed to Evict Them For Me / Southern Real Estate Services Group, LLC at info@evictthemforme.com or (404) 860-2444.
